# Privacy Policy for LinkedIn Engage AI

**Last Updated: December 15, 2025**

## Introduction

LinkedIn Engage AI ("we," "our," or "the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that helps users generate AI-powered engagement responses for LinkedIn. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Extension.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Google Account Information
When you sign in with Google, we collect:
- **Email address** - Used for authentication and identifying your account
- **Display name** - Used to personalize your experience
- **Profile picture** - Used to display your avatar in the Extension
- **Google User ID** - Used as a unique identifier for your account

### 2. Usage Data
We collect anonymous usage statistics including:
- Number of comments generated
- Types of engagement tones used
- Extension feature usage

### 3. LinkedIn Post Content
When you use the Extension, we temporarily process:
- The text content of LinkedIn posts you choose to engage with
- This content is sent to OpenAI's API to generate responses
- We do not store this content on our servers

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- **Authenticate** your access to the Extension
- **Generate** AI-powered engagement responses using OpenAI's GPT-4 API
- **Improve** the Extension's features and user experience
- **Track** usage statistics to understand feature popularity

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- Your authentication token is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage API
- Your usage statistics are stored locally on your device

### Cloud Storage (Firebase Firestore)
- Basic account information (email, name, profile picture, sign-in time) is stored in Firebase Firestore
- This data is used to maintain your account across sessions

## Third-Party Services

### OpenAI
- LinkedIn post content is sent to OpenAI's API to generate responses
- OpenAI's privacy policy applies: https://openai.com/privacy/

### Google Sign-In
- We use Google OAuth 2.0 for authentication
- Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Firebase (Google Cloud)
- User account data is stored in Firebase Firestore
- Firebase privacy policy applies: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for purposes other than those described in this policy

We **DO** share data with:
- **OpenAI** - Post content for generating AI responses (processed, not stored)
- **Firebase** - Account information for authentication purposes

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
- OAuth 2.0 authentication protocol
- Chrome's secure storage APIs for local data
- Firebase security rules to protect cloud data

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data stored by the Extension
- **Delete** your account and associated data
- **Opt-out** by uninstalling the Extension
- **Request** information about data we hold about you

To exercise these rights, contact us at: cmuthya001@gmail.com

## Data Retention

- Local data is retained until you uninstall the Extension or clear browser data
- Cloud data is retained until you request deletion
- We do not retain LinkedIn post content after processing

## Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
